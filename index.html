Welcome to SK Realty Management

SK Realty Management ("SKRM") is an opportunistic real estate investment group that has specialized in the purchase of warehouse distributions centers located in the Northeast and Midwest. SKRM has focused on acquiring two distinct asset types:a) stabilized core assets with cash-on-cash yields averaging above 15%b) opportunistic event-driven transactions with internal rate of returns in excess of 20%.Since its inception, SKRM has delivered transaction returns of over 50% on non-core assets. SKRM invests and manages real estate assets for a select group of major domestic opportunity funds, including, Angelo Gordon, Walton Street, and Blue Vista Capital Partners as well as several high net worth individuals. SKRM's current portfolio is in excess of $150,000,000 of real estate assets.

Market Opportunity SKRM believes that current market conditions provide a rewarding investment environment for it to continue its acquisition of warehouse distribution centers. SKRM's unique relationships with sales and leasing brokers (as well as regional property owners) have enabled SKRM to preemptively purchase assets before they have come to the market for sale. This, combined with SKRM's extensive network of debt providers, has created a favorable climate to achieve above market returns for its investments.
